## Ingredients

* 0.1 kg Crystal light malt

* 10 g Fuggles hops (boil 10 min)
* 10 g East Kent Goldings hops (boil 5 min)
* 15 g Fuggles hops (dry hop 3 days)

* 8.5 litre water (mash)
* 5.5 litre water (sparge)
* 1/4 teaspoon Irish moss
